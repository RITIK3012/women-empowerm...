About Us

Our Mission


Our mission is to empower women with tools to work their way out of poverty, care for their families, and strengthen their communities.

Our Work


We provide vital grants to high-impact, vetted nonprofit partners implementing economic empowerment programs for women experiencing poverty, locally and around the world.

Our Vision


We envision a world without poverty in which women are empowered, uplifted, and equal partners in society. Women live in safe, sustainable environments that include adequate food, water, housing, education, and health care. They have the hope, opportunity, and ability to control their lives and futures.
